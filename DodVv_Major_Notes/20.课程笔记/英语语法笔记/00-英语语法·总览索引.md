## 🎯 高频考点速查

### 动词时态——送分题

```diff
+ If it _____ tomorrow, we will cancel the class.
  A. rains    B. will rain    C. rained
  → 答案：A（主将从现）
```

### 定语从句 that vs which

```diff
+ This is the very book _____ I want to buy.
  → that（the very 只能用 that）
+ This is the town _____ I live.
  → in which（介词 + which）
```

### 非谓语动词——看逻辑主语

```diff
+ _____ from the mountain, we see the city.
  → Seeing（we 主动看）
+ _____ from the mountain, the city looks beautiful.
  → Seen（city 被看）
```

### 同位语从句 vs 定语从句——看 that 是否作成分

```diff
+ The news that he told me is true.     → 定语从句（that 作 told 的宾语）
+ The news that he passed the exam is true.  → 同位语从句（that 不作成分）
```
